About

I'm a medical doctor with an MBBS and clinical experience across pediatrics, pulmonology, and pre-operative assessments. My focus is on translating complex medical information into clear, actionable guidance for patients, families, and healthcare organizations.

Services

I offer clinical consultations for patients seeking clarity on diagnoses, treatment options, and second opinions. My approach emphasizes shared decision-making and ensures you feel confident about your healthcare choices.

For healthcare organizations, I provide strategic advisory services on medical communication, patient education materials, regulatory compliance, and clinical governance. This includes work with hospitals, pharmaceutical companies, digital health platforms, and government health bodies.

I also deliver workshops and talks on medical decision-making, risk communication, and healthcare policy for clinical teams, trainees, and health organizations.

Insights

I regularly write about clinical practice, healthcare systems, medical decision-making, and the art of clear communication in medicine. These insights are designed to help clinicians, patients, and healthcare leaders think more clearly about diagnosis, risk, and treatment.

Topics I explore include evidence-based medicine, shared decision-making, pharmaceutical regulation, health policy, and the challenges of communicating uncertainty in clinical settings.
