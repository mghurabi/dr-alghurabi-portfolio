Hi. I'm Mohammed Al-Ghurabi. I write about ideas. And I make the complex clear.

About

This is my personal space on the internet. A place where I explore ideas, share what I'm learning, and try to make sense of things that interest me.

I write about behavior, systems, decision-making, and the patterns that shape how we think and act. Sometimes about books I've read, sometimes about observations from everyday life.

This isn't a professional site. It's a notebook for my thoughts—a way to clarify my thinking by writing it down and sharing it with others who might find it useful.

Articles

I write occasional articles exploring ideas that interest me—usually around decision-making, systems thinking, behavioral patterns, and how we make sense of complexity.

These pieces are attempts to think through problems clearly, connect ideas from different domains, and share what I'm learning along the way.

Summaries

When I read a book or article that challenges my thinking, I try to distill its core ideas into a short summary. These aren't comprehensive reviews—they're my attempt to capture what resonated most.

The act of summarizing forces clarity. It's a way to test whether I've truly understood something by explaining it simply.

Talks

Occasionally, I speak about the ideas I explore in my writing. These talks are opportunities to engage with people directly, test ideas in conversation, and learn from questions I haven't considered.

If you're interested in having me speak at an event or workshop, feel free to reach out.
